                                                  RULES OF BLACKJACK SUPREME:
1. ONLY 1 PLAYER CAN PLAY AGAINST THE DEALER(COMPUTER)
2. MOST OF THE RULES ARE SAME AS REGULAR BLACKJACK GAME BARRING ONE OR TWO VARIATIONS
3. IN THIS VERSION OF BLACKJACK ONLY HIT AND STAY ARE ALLOWED
4. IF YOU GET A BLACKJACK WITH THE FIRST TWO CARDS ITSELF THEN DELAER WILL NOT PLAY ITS CARD AND YOU WILL WIN 1.5 TIMES YOUR BET
5. IN ALL OTHER CASES ON WINNING YOU WILL GET 2 TIMES YOUR BET
6. THERE IS NO MINIMUM BET
